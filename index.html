ABOUT

Francis Albert Sinatra (Born December 12, 1915 - May 14, 1998) was an American singer, actor, and producer, and was one of the most influential musical artists of the 20th century, along with being one of the best-selling music artists of all time. He found success as a solo artist with Columbia Records, traveled with the Rat Pack, and even came out of retirement with songs at the ready.

The best revenge is massive success.
